About Me

Hello there! I’m Muhammad, a front-end web developer based in Toronto. I combine my passion for technology and problem-solving to create fast, responsive, and accessible applications.

I bring over 5 years of prior experience from the world of accounting and finance leading teams and helping clients solve challenging problems on a daily basis.

The spark for my journey into the world of tech began many years ago as I developed a strong interest in computer science and programming - arising primariy out of my pursuit of finding innovative and efficient ways to solve problems.

I began to teach myself how to code intermittently for several years and subsequently decided to supercharge my technical abilities by completing Juno College’s Web Dev Immersive Bootcamp.

When I’m not on my laptop you’ll find me playing or watching basketball, spending time with family, or planning the next adventure trip with my friends!
